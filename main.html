Movie Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), a thief who enters the dreams of others to steal secrets from their subconscious. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform an "inception"—implanting an idea into someone’s mind without them realizing it. As Cobb and his team work to achieve the impossible, they are faced with challenges from both the subconscious minds they infiltrate and Cobb's own troubled past.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb: A skilled thief specializing in the art of "extraction," stealing secrets from deep within a person's subconscious.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur: Cobb’s trusted partner, responsible for organizing and executing the heist missions.

Ellen Page (now Elliot Page) as Ariadne: A talented architecture student who creates the dream worlds for the team to navigate.

Tom Hardy as Eames: A forger who can manipulate his appearance within dreams.
